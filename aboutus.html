About Us

Welcome to Cakes & Boba, a project developed as part of the Web Application Development course at SLIIT. Our goal is to create a user-friendly platform for exploring and purchasing delicious cakes and refreshing boba tea.
This project is designed with creativity, attention to detail, and a passion for good food. It demonstrates the use of modern web technologies to deliver a seamless and interactive user experience.
We hope you enjoy browsing through our offerings and appreciate the effort that went into building this project!
